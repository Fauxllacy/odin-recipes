Coconut Cream Pie

One slice of this coconut cream pie will have you soaring through the clouds.

Want this whole pie for yourself? You got it! All you need to achieve this recipe of great need are the following:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sweetened flaked coconut

  • 3 cups half-and-half

  • 3/4 cup white sugar

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

  • 2 large eggs, beaten

  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1 (9 inch) pie shell, baked

  • 1 cup frozen whipped topping, thawed

Steps:

  1. Start yourself off right by pre-heating your oven to 350 degrees.

  2. Get yourself a nice thin baking sheet and spread the coconut flakes evenly across the sheet.

  3. While the oven is pre-heating, take your coconut covered baking sheet and stir the flakes occasionally until golden brown for 5 minutes.

  4. Take your half-and-half, sugar, flour, eggs and salt and throw them into a medium sized cauldron, I mean pot. Bring the pot to a boil over low heat and stir occasionally. Keep stirring for 2 minutes.

  5. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in 3/4 cups of toasted coconut flakes and vanilla extract; save the rest of the toasted coconut flakes for the top fo the pie. Or don't. I won't tell if you won't tell.

  6. Pour the custard into the pie shell and let it chill until it's firm. This should take a good 4 hours so cozy up some place nice and away from the pie. I repeat, away from the pie.

  7. Top it off with whipped topping and sprinkle with the left over toasted coconut flakes to serve.
