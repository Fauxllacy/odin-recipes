Chocolate Pots de Creme

Creme de la who is putting Chocolate into Pots?

If there were a chocolate best suited in being the cream of the crop, it would be Chocolate Pots de Creme. Here is what you will need:

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups whipping cream (Heavy cream & Double Cream optional)

  • 12 ounces bittersweet/dark chocolate

  • 2 tbs sugar

  • 4 egg yolks

  • 1 tsp vanilla

  • 1 tsp coffee granules

  • 1 pinch kosher salt

Steps:

  1. In a small saucepan or microwave, heat up the cream until its hot; not boiling.

  2. Once hot, pour the cream over chocolate, coffee, sugar and vanilla. Let is stand for 5 minutes.

  3. Whisk the egg yolks and then add the cooled chocolate cream mixture.

  4. Divide the mixture into dessert dishes and let it chill in the fridge covered with plastic wrap for 1 hour.

  5. Serve and Enjoy!
